I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at Georgia Tech under the supervision of Professor Alan Ritter. I received my M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Chicago, where I also worked with Professor Karen Livescu and Professor Kevin Gimpel at TTIC. Before coming to the U.S., I received my bachelor degree (Hons) from the University of Melbourne in Australia.

Research Interests: Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning. I mainly work on multilingual NLP research. My goal is to build intelligent machines that help people with different cultural and language backgrounds.
